In this project, first added the dependencies at the time of project creation in STS( Spring Tool Suite).
Here is the dependency: Eureka Server
After click on finish to create the project, annotate the @EnableEurekaServer to act as a Eureka server.
open App.properties in the resource folder, add these properties
eureka.client.register-with-eureka=false 
eureka.client.fetch-registry=false
NOTE: If you are not allowed the self registry, otherwise don't mention these points in app.properties
